3D

If present, the 3D menu allows users to select the feedback that is displayed in a 3D application. It is an extension of the basic section viewer. Typical 3D feedback would be:

  • A coloured border defining the section and identifying its section viewer.
  • A coloured rectangle defining the section and identifying its section viewer.
  • A rectangle showing the 2D image of the section and having a coloured border to identify its section viewer.
  • An arrow showing the viewing direction for a particular section .
