show/intersection of views

a checkbox which toggles the visibility of intersection lines indicating the intersection of this view with other currently open views

  • the colour of an intersection line reflects the colour of the 'colour-chooser' control on the relevant section view.
  • adjusting the distance control and/or the rotation controls for a particular section view will cause the appropriate intersection line to move in other open views. (Provided that the 'intersection of views' check-box is selected.)
