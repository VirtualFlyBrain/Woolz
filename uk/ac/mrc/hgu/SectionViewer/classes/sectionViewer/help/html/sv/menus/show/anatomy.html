show/'mouse-click' anatomy

a checkbox which toggles the visibility of anatomy feedback for 3D images whose anatomy components have been 'painted'.

  • anatomy feedback is obtained by clicking or dragging the mouse in the 2D section.
  • if an anatomy component exists at the mouse position it will be highlighted in magenta.
  • the full path name of the anatomy component will be displayed in the anatomy feedback area.
  • the text in the anatomy feedback area may be scrolled by clicking and dragging the mouse on the text.
