Anatomy Key

The Anatomy key indicates the colour with which a component is highlighted in section views. It also gives the (fully qualified) name of the component.

The Anatomy Key is launched when an item of anatomy is selected from the Anatomy menu of the 3D viewer. It may also be launched from this menu by selecting show key.

Only one Key will be launched.

The Key has 6 rows, each comprising ...

  • a coloured panel
  • left/right arrow buttons
  • a scrollable text field
  • a visibility checkbox
  • a zap/replace button

The 6 preset colours are ...

  1. brown
  2. red
  3. yellow
  4. blue
  5. green
  6. cyan

If more than 6 items of anatomy are selected, the original items are replaced starting with item #1.
