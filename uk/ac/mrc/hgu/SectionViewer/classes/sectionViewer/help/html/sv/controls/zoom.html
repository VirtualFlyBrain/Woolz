zoom

  • The zoom control comprises:
    • The "zoom %" label
    • A text field that displays the current zoom in terms of % magnification
    • - and + buttons
  • Zoom can be adjusted by means of the - and + buttons, or by editing the text field.
    (return or enter must be pressed for any change to take effect)
  • The - and + buttons change zoom in steps of 50%.
  • Zoom is initially adjustable between 20% and 1000%.
  • The text field can be used to select any value of zoom.
