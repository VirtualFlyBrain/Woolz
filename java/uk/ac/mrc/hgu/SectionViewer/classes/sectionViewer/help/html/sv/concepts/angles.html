yaw pitch roll

  • Yaw is rotation around the Z axis (vertical).
  • Pitch is rotation around the X axis (horizontal, accross the screen).
  • Roll is rotation around the Y axis (horizontal, in and out of the screen).

The sliders for these angles show values in degrees (although the programme works in radians).
